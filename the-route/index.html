The Route

Vietnam, that’s where I’m heading (as part of the first phase). Let’s not get ahead of myself eh?

South-east through Europe via Greece to see Captain Hood and his seafaring vessels, over to Turkey, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan (reprise), Krygyzstan, China, Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, and Vietnam. Then I’m going to stop, eat loads, drink loads, get fat, then, and only then I’ll think about continuing this bike ride.

In the meantime I’ve found this lovely site called Tripline, which lets me log where I’ve been up until then last time I’ve managed rob internet from somewhere.
