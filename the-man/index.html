The Man

Hello. I’m Sam.

At 09.33 on the 28th of May 2015 I packed up my bags, hooked them onto the sides of my trusty cycle; The Tangerine Dream, and set off cycling South East. I’m not sure how far I’ll get, I’m not sure exactly where I’ll go or end up, but hopefully I’ll see a lot of fun things and post the evidence up here.
